On October 31, 2024, Second Lieutenant Thanu Wongchinda, Secretary-General of the Basic Education Commission (OBEC Secretary-General), attended the opening ceremony of the "Suan Exhibition 2024", the 13th Suankularb Wittayalai Academic Exhibition, with Police General. Permpoon Chidchob, Minister of Education (Minister of Education), presided over the ceremony along with the administrators, teachers, educational personnel, dignitaries, and students, they attended at the center stage of the football field at Suankularb Wittayalai School. Bangkok

The Suankularb Wittayalai Academic Exhibition or Suankularb Wittayalai Academic Exhibition is an exhibition that showcases the perspectives, ideas, and abilities of students of Suankularb Wittayalai School. It is the only major event of the school that is open to the public. It is scheduled to be held every 4 years. The latest Suankularb Wittayalai Exhibition 2021 was postponed indefinitely due to the COVID-19 pandemic, causing Suankularb Wittayalai School to be absent from organizing the exhibition for 8 years. This Suankularb Wittayalai Exhibition 2024 has the theme of "Unlimited Dreams Company (Youth): Dream Corp." which talks about the dreams of youth that are gathered in a company that creates dreams, collects dreams, and displays them, allowing participants to get to know themselves better through an exhibition that focuses on creating an experience of visiting the school's interior. Every building, every floor, and every room is designed to be a room for exhibitions, performances, and various activities, making Suankularb Wittayalai School a true dreamland, thanks to the work of students from all levels, more than 50 clubs.

Examples of spectacular exhibitions include the Bangkok Flooding Exhibition from the Geography and Earth Science Club, an exhibition that allows everyone to receive an immersive experience of real water, real falling, real wetness, to raise awareness of the global warming crisis that is unknowingly destroying our world. Or the Blindfolded Room Exhibition from the Mystical Science Club. At first glance, Suankularb School may be a school that excels in academics and activities, but a unique club like Mystical Science can bring out an exhibition that talks about human fears as a unique experience that cannot be found anywhere else.

There will also be concerts from 12 famous artists in the evening, including Khunpol & Peemwasu from BUS because of you shine, PUN, Anatomy Rabbit, MEAN, YEW, FREEHAND, Bedroom Audio, V3RSE, Yes Indeed, SLUR, The Parkinson and Musketeers. The 2024 Suankularb Wittayalai Exhibition is free to attend, from October 31 - November 2, 2024, for a total of three full days, from 8:00 a.m. to 7:00 p.m. at Suankularb Wittayalai School.
